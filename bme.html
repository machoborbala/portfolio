In 2023, a project was started to relaunch the BME's websites and update its branding. The design involved UX researchers, UI designers and website developers. The university also involved students and after an interview I was lucky enough to join the UI design team. As part of the team, I created the design of the individual pages and elements of the website in Figma, and my tasks included creating wireframes, clickable prototypes and graphics, and designing the layout and content of the pages.

Design process

At the beginning of the design process, we familiarized ourselves with the already created Figma library, the styles we wanted to use and the main brand elements. The goal was to create a more youthful, clean website that all members of the target audience could easily navigate. The library contained the colours, font styles, gaps, padding and margin sizes that could be used for desktop and mobile view. Based on this, we first created the components and their variants that would be used repeatedly on the pages, such as buttons, cards, header and footer.
